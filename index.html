Scoring criteria — full reference
C1 — H4 Trnd (+20 pts)
HH/HL bullish or LL/LH bearish. Impulsive moves — direction obvious in 3 seconds.
C2 — H4 Str (+10 pts)
Clean swing highs/lows. Strong displacement legs between swings. Minimal fake breakouts.
C3 — H4 D&S (+15 pts)
Entry at meaningful H4 level — prior swing H/L, strong rejection zone, aggressive departure area.
C4 — Liq (+10 pts)
Equal highs/lows or session H/L. Liquidity just taken OR about to be taken before entry.
C5 — Pr Zn Brk (+5 pts)
Previous move from zone was fast and aggressive. Large candles / clear displacement present.
C6 — M5 BOS (+10 pts)
Clear BOS or CHoCH on M5 confirming direction after reaching HTF level. Not anticipatory.
C7 — M5 BO (+5 pts)
Strong move in entry direction. Clear intent — not weak or overlapping candles.
C8 — Entry (+5 pts)
Tight stop loss relative to structure. Not chasing price. Stop placed at structural point.
C9 — Sess (+5 pts)
Trade occurs during Asian or London session only.
C10 — Mkt Act (+5 pts)
Not near session close. Market is active — not dead or low volatility.
C11 — R:R (+5 pts)
Minimum 3:1 R:R confirmed before entry. Measure to next HTF level.
C12 — Path (+5 pts)
No opposing HTF level or choppy consolidation between entry and target.
P1 — HTF↓ (−15 pts)
Trading INTO HTF level. Entering against the key level direction.
P2 — News (−10 pts)
High-impact news imminent. Check economic calendar before every session.
P3 — Chop (−10 pts)
Choppy / unclear structure (H4). Constant break-and-fail. No clear swing direction.
P4 — Chase (−10 pts)
Entry far from key level. Chasing price. Stop wider than structure requires.
